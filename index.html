About Us

Sagun Capital, founded by Saket Agarwal, is a multi-asset Family Office investing exclusively in India. For over 25 years, we’ve backed transformative businesses across sectors using only proprietary capital — with a presence in public equities, private equity, and early-stage startups. Our investments reflect a deep conviction in long-term value creation and a belief in the India growth story.

Investment Philosophy

We seek enduring value. Every investment reflects long-term thinking, discipline, and deep research. As sector-agnostic investors, we back high-quality teams building for India's future — whether in traditional industries or frontier technologies.
